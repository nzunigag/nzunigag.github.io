About me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Transportation Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin working with Dr. Randy Machemehl. I hold a master's in Statistics and a master's in Civil Engineering from UT Austin. I received a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering from the University of Costa Rica, where I served as interim professor and researcher before joining UT Austin for graduate school.
I am interested in the applications of data analytics and AI/machine learning methods in transportation engineering and bridging the gap between these areas to provide planning and operations solutions. In addition to my academic work, I am involved in mentoring activities that promote STEM careers to new generations. I occupy leading positions in student and community organizations.
