About

Natalia is a Ph.D. candidate in Transportation Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin working with Dr. Randy Machemehl. She holds a master in Statistics and a master in Civil Engineering from UT Austin. She received her B.Sc. in Civil Engineering from the University of Costa Rica, where she served as an interim professor and researcher before joining UT Austin for graduate school.

Natalia is interested in the applications of data analytics and AI/machine learning methods in transportation engineering and bridging the gap between these areas to provide planning and operations solutions. In addition to her academic work, Natalia is involved in mentoring activities that promote STEM careers to new generations. She occupies leading positions in student and community organizations.
